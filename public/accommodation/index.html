Selected partner hotels

JA Experiences & Discovery is developing a controlled hotel-room-only partner model. No selected partner hotels are currently confirmed.

Current position

No hotel room payments are being accepted

JA Group Services Ltd will only sell agreed hotel-room-only availability where the relevant hotel has provided suitable written commercial rights, supplier authority or partner permission.

Until that approval and the required operational controls are in place, customers must book and pay the hotel or relevant third-party platform directly.

The model will not include

  • Package holidays
  • Flights or flight-inclusive travel
  • Visas or immigration services
  • Transfers, coaches, ferries or trains
  • Taxis, cabs or car hire
  • Any combined transport and hotel booking
Operational readiness

Customer protection and operational readiness come first

Hotel-room-only sales will begin only after supplier agreements, customer terms, payment and reconciliation controls, cancellation and refund processes, complaints handling, privacy wording, tax review and insurance review are ready.
