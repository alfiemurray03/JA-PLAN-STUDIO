Formal Board Notification: JA Experiences & Discovery - Director's Platform Direction

This page is an internal governance reference and is not part of public navigation.

1. Purpose of this Board Notification

To formally record the intended direction and action for JA Experiences & Discovery as a subscription-based self-service member platform.

2. Authority and Responsibility

The director is setting the platform direction and implementation priorities for the service, subject to legal, operational, customer and data protection responsibilities.

3. Relationship with the Former Board-Approved Direction

The former manual travel planning and guidance direction is superseded where it conflicts with the new self-service platform model.

4. Strategic Direction

JA Experiences & Discovery will operate as a member platform for guided builders, templates, checklists, discovery boards, saved plans and planning tools.

5. Operational Deadline

The operational target for the full platform direction is Mid-September 2026.

6. Paid Subscription Model

The platform will use paid monthly subscriptions and will not offer a permanent free plan or unlimited free builder usage.

7. 14-Day Free Trial Access Token

One limited 14-day free trial token may be activated once per customer account, includes 30 Builder Usage Tokens once only, and expires automatically exactly 14 days after activation.

8. Builder Usage Tokens System

Builder Usage Tokens apply to self-service platform tools and are deducted when finished outputs are created, saved, generated or completed.

9. Subscription Plans

Plans include JA Experience Builder Membership, Plus and Family with monthly credit allowances of 150, 350 and 750 Builder Usage Tokens respectively.

10. Experience Builders

Builders will cover everyday experiences, travel readiness, accessibility/support planning and organisation tools.

11. Admin Controls

Administration controls are required for builders, plans, credit allowances, costs, inclusion, usage, blocked attempts, purchases, add-ons and manual credit adjustments.

12. Usage Limits and Enforcement

Completion of builder outputs must check active subscription or trial status, plan inclusion, credit availability and restrictions before deducting credits.

13. Extra Credits and Paid Add-Ons

Extra Builder Usage Tokens and human support add-ons are separate paid services and must not be mixed together.

14. Affiliate and Partner Position

Headout and GetYourGuide remain third-party affiliate discovery partners. Bookings are made directly with the relevant provider.

15. Customer Responsibility and Service Boundaries

Customers remain responsible for prices, opening times, availability, accessibility, suitability, travel arrangements, official guidance, booking terms and provider information. JA Group Services Ltd does not sell package holidays or manage third-party bookings.

16. Implementation Position

The platform controls and user experience should be implemented in safe phases without weakening authentication, customer data protections, auditability or payment boundaries.

17. Intended Outcome

JA Experiences & Discovery should become a premium, self-service member platform for guided experience planning and account-managed usage.

This notification is issued as formal written notification to the Board of the intended direction and action for JA Experiences & Discovery.
