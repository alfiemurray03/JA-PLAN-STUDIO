Pre-launch model

Discovery, planning support and clearly labelled external providers

The following model is being developed. Public booking, customer accounts and direct support services are not yet live.

1

Explore and research

Customers may use future destination, activity, transport and accommodation-area guidance to develop their own ideas.

2

Save and organise

Future account tools may support favourites, travel notes and customer-created itineraries.

3

Request JA support

JA Group Services Ltd may later offer directly supplied planning, research and information support under clear service terms.

4

Choose an external provider

Where partner links are introduced, customers will book directly with the selected provider and must review its terms.

Not package travel or a live holiday booking service

JA Group Services Ltd is not currently selling holidays, flights, insurance, accommodation, transfers, tours, activities or package travel in its own name. The platform must not be understood as a travel agent, tour operator or package holiday provider.
