How it works

A simple guided workflow

1. Sign in

Use JA Group Services ID to open your protected customer account.

2. Choose

Select an available builder based on its purpose and intended outcome.

3. Complete

Answer focused questions and review the structured plan.

4. Manage

Save and revisit supported builder work securely inside your account.
