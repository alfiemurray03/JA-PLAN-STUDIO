Service boundary

Transfers and transport bookings

JA Experiences & Discovery does not sell, arrange or take payment for transfers or transport.

Excluded services: flights, airport transfers, private transfers, coaches, ferries, trains, taxis, cabs, car hire and any other transport booking.

What JA can do

A guidance plan may identify official transport sources, general journey considerations and questions to ask. You remain responsible for choosing, booking and paying the transport provider.
