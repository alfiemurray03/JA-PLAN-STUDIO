Affiliate discovery

Third-party experiences stay clearly separate.

Some links or widgets may direct members to third-party providers, including Headout and GetYourGuide. JA Group Services Ltd may receive commission from qualifying bookings. Bookings are made directly with the relevant third-party provider and are subject to that provider's own terms, conditions, cancellation rules, refund rules and privacy notice.

Member responsibility

Check provider details before booking.

Members are responsible for checking prices, opening times, availability, accessibility, suitability, travel arrangements, official guidance, booking terms and provider information.
