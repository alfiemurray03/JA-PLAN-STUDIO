Important scope

What these plans do not include

  • JA Group Services Ltd does not sell flights or package holidays.
  • JA Group Services Ltd does not arrange transport, transfers, taxis, coaches, ferries, trains, car hire or flights.
  • Affiliate activity bookings are made directly with the relevant third-party provider.
  • Hotel-room-only bookings are not available unless written supplier or partner authority is in place.
