Before you pay

We confirm the right scope first.

Paid guidance should only begin where the plan fits your request. If your enquiry needs something outside our service scope, we will tell you.

1

Submit a free enquiry

Tell us what you need, including dates, destination ideas and budget.

2

We review the request

We consider whether a guidance plan is suitable for your enquiry.

3

You choose whether to continue

No paid work starts until the paid route is confirmed.

Minor revisions

Small changes are different from a new request.

A minor revision means a small adjustment to the guidance provided. Major changes, such as changing the destination, dates, traveller group or overall request, may require a new enquiry or additional paid service.

Important

You remain in control of bookings.

JA Experiences & Discovery provides guidance, research and signposting. Customers remain responsible for making and managing their own travel, transport and third-party provider arrangements.
