Data protection

Privacy Notice

How JA Experiences & Discovery uses personal data when you browse the website, submit an enquiry, create an account or use customer support.

Who we are

JA Experiences & Discovery is a service line of JA Group Services Ltd. JA Group Services Ltd acts as Data Controller for the personal data processed through this website and associated customer services.

What we collect

We may collect identity details, contact information, account information, enquiry content, support requests, membership details, notification preferences, billing references, and technical records such as device and session metadata. Where you choose to provide accessibility, disability, medical, family or carer information, we only use it to support your request.

Why we use it

We use personal data to operate customer accounts, respond to enquiries, provide guidance services, manage support cases, maintain audit records, process membership and billing administration, comply with legal obligations, and protect the security of the service.

Lawful bases

Depending on the context, we rely on contract, legitimate interests, consent, legal obligation and vital interests where applicable. Consent is only used where it is genuinely required and can be withdrawn at any time.

Sharing

We share information with service providers only where needed to deliver the service, operate the platform, process payments, send emails, or comply with legal obligations. We also use Microsoft Entra External ID and related Microsoft services to manage authentication and account identity.

International transfers

Some suppliers may process information outside the United Kingdom. Where this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards and contractual measures where required.

Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purpose collected, to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain support history and preserve audit records.

Your rights

You may have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and portability under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Contact

For privacy queries, contact dataprotection@jagroupservices.co.uk.
