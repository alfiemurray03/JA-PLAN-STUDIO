Who we are

What we collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity Details: Name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Information: Email address and telephone numbers.
  • Account Information: Passwords, security question answers, and verification PINs.
  • Enquiry Content: Details of destinations, experiences, and support needs you share with us.
  • Technical Data: IP address, login data, browser type, and device information.

"Where you choose to provide special category data (such as health or accessibility needs), we process this only with your explicit consent to support your specific request."

Why we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide our services and respond to your enquiries.
  • Manage and secure your customer account.
  • Perform internal audits and maintain security (including logging administrator access).
  • Process payments and manage memberships.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with service providers only where needed to deliver our service:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Cloudflare (hosting, database, security).
  • Identity Management: Microsoft (authentication and account services).
  • Communication: Email service providers for transaction notifications.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have rights including:

Access

You have the right to ask us for copies of your personal information.

Rectification

You have the right to ask us to rectify information you think is inaccurate.

Erasure

You have the right to ask us to erase your personal information in certain circumstances.

Portability

You have the right to ask that we transfer the information you gave us to another organisation.

You can exercise these rights through your Customer Account or by contacting us directly.
