Important information

Travel insurance

JA Group Services Ltd does not sell, arrange or recommend a specific insurance policy through this service.

Consider appropriate cover

Customers should consider suitable insurance for their circumstances, destination and planned activities. Read policy terms, exclusions, excesses and medical disclosure requirements carefully.

Independent decision

Use an authorised insurer, broker or comparison service and check the Financial Conduct Authority register where appropriate. JA does not provide regulated insurance advice.

Activity cover

Some activities may require specialist cover or be excluded from standard policies. Confirm this before booking the activity.
