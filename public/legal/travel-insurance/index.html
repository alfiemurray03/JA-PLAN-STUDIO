Independent Decision

You should use an authorised insurer, broker or comparison service. Check the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) register where appropriate. JA does not provide regulated insurance advice.

Appropriate Cover

Consider suitable insurance for your circumstances, destination and planned activities. Read policy terms, exclusions, excesses and medical disclosure requirements carefully.

Activity Cover

Some activities may require specialist cover or be excluded from standard policies. Confirm this before booking the activity with your provider.
