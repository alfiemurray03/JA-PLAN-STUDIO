Cookie Policy

How JA Experiences & Discovery uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

What cookies are

Cookies are small files placed on your device by your browser. They help the site function properly and can be used to remember preferences or measure website performance.

Cookies we use

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication, security, route protection, load balancing and session continuity. We may also use preference cookies to remember display choices and accessibility settings. Any non-essential analytics or marketing cookies should only be used where allowed and configured.

Why we use them

Cookies help us keep the customer portal secure, maintain sign-in state, support user preferences and operate the website reliably. Necessary cookies are required for the platform to function.

Third-party cookies

Where a third-party service is used, such as an embedded provider or payment flow, that service may place its own cookies in line with its own policies and controls.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect sign-in, security or site behaviour.
