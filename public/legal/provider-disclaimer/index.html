Travel & Provider Disclaimer

Important limitations relating to travel information, accessibility and independent providers.

Research, not a travel booking

JA Experiences & Discovery provides research, planning support and general destination information. We do not sell or operate travel services and do not become a party to a customer's contract with a flight, accommodation, attraction, transport, insurance or other provider.

Changing information

Prices, schedules, entry rules, availability, local laws, safety conditions and provider terms can change after research is completed. Customers must check official advice and confirm important facts directly before booking and travelling.

Accessibility

Accessibility information is based on public descriptions and provider responses where available. We do not inspect venues and cannot guarantee that facilities will meet individual requirements.

Official Advice

For international travel, check the latest UK Government foreign travel advice, relevant embassy or immigration information and current health guidance.

Emergencies

We do not provide emergency or 24-hour in-destination support. Contact local emergency services, the travel provider, insurer or official consular assistance as appropriate.
