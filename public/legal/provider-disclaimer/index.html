Travel and third-party provider disclaimer

Last updated: 11 June 2026

Research, not a travel booking

JA Experiences & Discovery provides research, planning support and general destination information. We do not sell or operate travel services and do not become a party to a customer's contract with a flight, accommodation, attraction, transport, insurance or other provider.

Changing information

Prices, schedules, entry rules, availability, local laws, safety conditions and provider terms can change after research is completed. Customers must check official advice and confirm important facts directly before booking and travelling.

Accessibility

Accessibility information is based on public descriptions and provider responses where available. We do not inspect venues and cannot guarantee that facilities, routes, vehicles, rooms or assistance will meet individual requirements. Customers should obtain direct written confirmation from providers where a requirement is essential.

Safety and suitability

No destination, route or activity can be guaranteed safe or suitable. Suggestions are planning ideas based on the information supplied, not a professional risk assessment. Customers must exercise their own judgement and obtain appropriate professional advice where needed.

Official advice

For international travel, check the latest UK Government foreign travel advice, relevant embassy or immigration information and current health guidance. JA does not decide whether a person meets entry requirements.

Affiliate links

Some clearly labelled links may be affiliate links. JA Group Services Ltd may receive commission after a qualifying booking. The customer still contracts directly with the provider, and the provider remains responsible for its service and support.

Emergencies

We do not provide emergency or 24-hour in-destination support. Contact local emergency services, the travel provider, insurer or official consular assistance as appropriate.
