Provider Disclaimer

The distinction between JA support and external travel providers.

JA Travel & Experiences is a trading name of JA Group Services Ltd and is not a separate legal entity. JA Group Services Ltd provides travel planning, research and information support. We do not operate flights, accommodation, tours, transfers, insurance or transport services unless expressly stated. Where customers choose to book with a third-party provider, the booking is made directly with that provider and is subject to the provider’s own terms, prices, availability, cancellation rules and customer support arrangements.

External provider responsibility

External providers are responsible for describing, supplying, operating and supporting their own services. JA Group Services Ltd cannot approve amendments, issue refunds, change provider policies or resolve operational matters on a provider’s behalf.

Information changes

Travel information, availability, pricing, schedules and entry requirements can change. Customers must verify important details with official sources and the relevant provider.

Affiliate relationships

JA Group Services Ltd may receive a commission where a customer clicks selected links and completes a booking with a third-party provider. This does not transfer provider responsibility to JA.
