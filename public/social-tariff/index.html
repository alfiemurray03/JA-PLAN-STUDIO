Eligibility

Who may request the social tariff?

Customers receiving Universal Credit, PIP, other accepted benefits or experiencing financial difficulty may ask to be considered.

Evidence may be requested

Basic evidence may be requested where needed to assess eligibility. Please do not upload or send benefit letters through the public enquiry form unless we specifically ask for them through an appropriate route.

Minimum information

We request only the minimum necessary information and handle any evidence carefully. We do not need unnecessary details about your wider circumstances.

How to request it

Use the free enquiry form and tick the social tariff option. We will explain the next step privately before any paid guidance plan starts.
