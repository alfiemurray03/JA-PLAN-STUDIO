FAQ

Common questions

What are Builder Usage Tokens?

Builder Usage Tokens are the monthly or trial allowance used when you save or complete finished builder outputs.

When are tokens deducted?

Tokens are deducted only after a finished output is saved or completed. Opening a builder and previewing your answers does not deduct tokens.

Can I preview before saving?

Yes. You can preview your output before deciding whether to save it.

What happens when my trial ends?

Your trial expires 14 days after activation and cannot be restarted. Choose a paid plan to continue using paid builder features.

Can I buy extra tokens?

Extra token add-ons are available for paid members when configured in your account.

Are bookings made through JA Plan Studio?

No. We help you plan and organise. Any third-party booking is completed with the provider under their terms.
