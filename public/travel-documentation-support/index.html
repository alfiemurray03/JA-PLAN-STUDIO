Travel Documentation Support

Administrative support for travel documents and entry authorisation checks.

We can help customers understand official travel-authorisation processes, prepare checklists and find official sources before they make their own decisions.

How we may help

Clear support for customers who find online processes difficult.

This service can be useful for customers who struggle with online forms, digital access, reading confidence, accessibility needs, disability, age-related support needs, anxiety or uncertainty.

Official-source signposting

Links and signposting to official government, border, consular or travel-authorisation sources.

Entry authorisation support

Administrative help understanding ETIAS, UK ETA where applicable and similar electronic travel authorisation processes.

Passport and document checklists

Checklist support for passport validity, destination entry requirement checks and documents customers may need to review.

Form confidence

General administrative assistance to help customers understand official online processes and the information they may need.

Preparation notes

Plain-English notes to help customers organise next steps before using official sources or authorised providers.

Minimum information

Customers should only provide information needed for the support requested. Sensitive, disability, medical, family or carer information is voluntary and handled carefully.

Important exclusion

We do not provide immigration, visa or legal advice.

JA Group Services Ltd does not provide immigration advice, visa advice, legal advice, nationality advice, residency advice, appeal support, refusal advice or representation before any government, border, consular, immigration or EU authority.

Decisions remain official

No approval guarantee

JA Group Services Ltd does not guarantee approval of any ETIAS, ETA, travel authorisation, visa waiver or similar application. Decisions remain with the relevant authority.
