Accessibility-aware guidance

We provide research shaped around the access information you choose to share, helping you identify accessible routes and venues.

What we research

  • Step-free routes and entrances
  • Accessible toilets and seating
  • Sensory and crowd considerations
  • Provider accessibility contacts

Important limitation

We do not inspect venues and cannot guarantee accessibility. Essential requirements must be confirmed directly with the provider.

Your information

Disability and medical details are handled carefully. Only share what is necessary for the research you need.

Website access

We aim for readable contrast and clear navigation. Report website issues to hello@jagroupservices.co.uk.
