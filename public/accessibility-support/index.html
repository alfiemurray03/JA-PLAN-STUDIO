Accessibility-aware guidance

Research shaped around the access information you choose to share.

We can look for publicly available accessibility information and help identify questions to confirm directly with providers.

What we can research

  • Step-free routes and entrances
  • Accessible toilets and seating
  • Mobility, sensory or crowd considerations
  • Provider contact details
  • Questions to ask before booking

Important limitation

We do not inspect venues and cannot guarantee that a room, attraction, route or activity will meet an individual's needs. Essential requirements must be confirmed directly with the provider, preferably in writing.

Your information

Only share information that is necessary to explain the research you need. Disability, medical and accessibility details can require special protection and are handled carefully.

Website access

We aim for keyboard-friendly navigation, readable contrast, clear language and responsive layouts. Report a website access problem to hello@jagroupservices.co.uk.
