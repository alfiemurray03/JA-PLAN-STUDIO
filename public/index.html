Affiliates and boundaries

Third-party booking stays separate.

Some links or widgets may direct members to third-party providers, including Headout and GetYourGuide. Bookings are made directly with the relevant third-party provider and are subject to that provider's own terms, conditions, cancellation rules, refund rules and privacy notice.

JA Group Services Ltd does not sell package holidays, arrange flights, arrange transport, apply for visas or ETIAS, manage third-party bookings, or handle third-party refunds or cancellations.
