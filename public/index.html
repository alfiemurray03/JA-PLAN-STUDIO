Pre-launch travel planning platform

Travel planning, research and discovery — built with structure.

JA Travel & Experiences is being developed as a travel discovery, planning and information platform by JA Group Services Ltd.

The platform is intended to help customers explore destinations, understand local transport, compare accommodation areas, discover activities and experiences, build itinerary ideas and request future planning support from JA.

Platform direction

More than affiliate links.

JA direct support

JA Group Services Ltd may in future provide direct travel planning, research and information support services, including destination research, itinerary planning, accommodation-area guidance, local transport guidance and pre-travel planning support.

These services are currently being scoped and are not yet open as a public purchase service.

Third-party partner options

Where tours, activities, accommodation, transfers or other travel services are introduced through third-party links, customers will book directly with the relevant external provider.

JA Group Services Ltd may receive commission where selected third-party links lead to completed bookings.

Important pre-launch notice: JA Travel & Experiences is a trading name of JA Group Services Ltd and is not a separate legal entity. JA Group Services Ltd provides travel planning, research and information support. We do not operate flights, accommodation, tours, transfers, insurance or transport services unless expressly stated. Where customers choose to book with a third-party provider, the booking is made directly with that provider and is subject to the provider’s own terms, prices, availability, cancellation rules and customer support arrangements.
