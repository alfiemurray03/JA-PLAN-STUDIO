Direct JA services

Travel support from JA Group Services Ltd.

We are developing direct travel planning and research support services, including itinerary planning, destination research, transfer guidance, local transport advice and pre-travel checklists.

Direct service note Direct JA travel support services are currently being scoped and are not yet open for public booking. Final service descriptions, pricing, availability and terms will be confirmed before launch.
1
Destination research

Helping customers understand where they are going, what to expect and what to consider.

2
Itinerary planning

Supporting customers with practical day-by-day planning ideas and travel flow.

3
Transport and transfer guidance

Helping explain airport routes, local transport options, trains, buses, metros and transfers.

4
Pre-travel checklists

Creating clear reminders for bookings, travel documents, timings and useful destination notes.

Partner bookings

Booking options will be introduced carefully.

Where third-party booking links or partner services are introduced, bookings will be completed with the relevant external provider and will be subject to that provider’s own terms, prices, availability, amendment rules and cancellation policies.

Not yet a live booking service The public booking journey is not currently open. Partner booking links, supplier details and affiliate disclosures will be reviewed before wider public launch.
