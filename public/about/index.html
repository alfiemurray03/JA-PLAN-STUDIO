About the Platform

Self-service planning for everyday experiences

JA Experiences & Discovery is a subscription-based platform that helps you build, organize, and manage your own plans using guided tools and smart templates.

EB

What we offer

A subscription platform with guided builders for days out, family activities, travel planning, accessibility considerations, and budget organization.

TC

How it works

Use Builder Usage Tokens to save finished plans. Preview outputs for free, and only spend credits when you're ready to save a completed plan.

SP

What you control

You remain responsible for checking prices, opening times, accessibility, provider terms, and making your own bookings.

Guided builders

Step-by-step builders help you create plans for family days out, travel experiences, special occasions, accessibility considerations, and budget planning.

All builders are self-service tools that generate planning outputs based on the information you provide.

Discovery tools

Discovery boards let you organize ideas, notes, budgets, and booking information in one place.

Use saved plans to revisit and update your outputs whenever you need.

What we don't do

We do not sell package holidays, arrange flights, arrange transport, apply for visas or ETIAS, manage third-party bookings, or handle third-party refunds.

Bookings and transactions with third-party providers are your responsibility.

Affiliate relationships

Some links may direct you to third-party providers. Bookings are made directly with those providers under their own terms.

JA Group Services Ltd does not process payments or handle bookings for third-party services.
