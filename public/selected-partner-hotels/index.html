Selected Partner Hotels information

There are currently no confirmed selected partner hotels, and hotel-room-only bookings are not available through JA Experiences & Discovery.

No confirmed partner hotels

JA Experiences & Discovery may only list or sell hotel-room-only bookings where a selected partner hotel has granted written commercial rights, supplier authority or partner permission.

No authority means no payment

If there is no written hotel resale or supplier right in place, JA Group Services Ltd will not take customer payment for that hotel room.

No checkout is available

This page is information only. It does not allow hotel room payment, hotel booking checkout or hotel-room-only sales.

Separate service area

Hotels are separate from activity affiliate bookings.

Selected Partner Hotels are kept separate from Headout and GetYourGuide activity affiliate bookings. JA Experiences & Discovery does not combine hotels and activities into a package holiday.

Customer responsibility

Customers remain responsible for their own travel, transport and practical arrangements before, during and after any trip.
