Build the plan in the right order

Choosing activities before understanding the destination, journey times and the people travelling often creates an unrealistic itinerary.

Choose the right destination

Compare the overall feel, realistic trip length, season, travel effort and the needs of everyone travelling.

Set your priorities

Identify the two or three experiences that matter most, then build around them rather than trying to fit everything in.

Shape each day

Group nearby places, allow for meals and rest, and avoid placing demanding activities immediately after arrival.

Confirm and book

Check current official advice and provider details, then make each booking directly with the relevant provider.

A useful plan should answer practical questions

It should explain where to focus, what can realistically fit, what needs advance booking and what still needs to be confirmed.

  • How much time is available each day?
  • Which experiences are fixed and which can stay flexible?
  • How long will local journeys take?
  • Where are breaks, meals and quieter time?
  • What access, age or health restrictions need checking?

Important checks before you commit

Official information

Check current entry, passport, health, safety and local-law information from authoritative sources.

Provider terms

Review price, availability, inclusions, exclusions, cancellation terms and meeting instructions.

Your own arrangements

JA does not arrange flights or transport. You remain responsible for reaching each destination, hotel, venue or activity.
