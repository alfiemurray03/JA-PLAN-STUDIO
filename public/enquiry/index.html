What the free enquiry covers

The Free Discovery Enquiry helps us understand your request and recommend a suitable guidance route.

It does not include detailed research, full itinerary preparation, destination comparison, hotel area research or cost breakdown work.

Our services are delivered online. Paid guidance documents may be supplied by email in Word, Excel and/or PDF format, depending on the service selected.
