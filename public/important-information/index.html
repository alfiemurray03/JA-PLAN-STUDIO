Important Information

Please understand the scope of our service before relying on guidance or booking with a third party.

JA Experiences & Discovery provides guidance, research, signposting and administrative support. It is not a flight, transport or package holiday provider.

Our service

JA Experiences & Discovery provides online destination discovery, activity guidance, itinerary and experience planning support, administrative travel documentation support, affiliate signposting and customer support.

No flights or package holidays

JA Group Services Ltd does not sell flights and does not sell package holidays. Services and third-party activity links must not be treated as a combined travel package.

No transport arranging

JA Group Services Ltd does not arrange transport, transfers, taxis, coaches, ferries, trains, car hire or flights. Customers remain responsible for their own travel and transport arrangements.

Third-party activities

Headout and GetYourGuide bookings are made with the relevant third-party provider. That provider is responsible for its own prices, availability, terms, refunds, cancellations and service delivery.

Travel documentation support

Documentation support is administrative support, checklist support, official-source signposting and help understanding official travel-authorisation processes only.

No immigration, visa or legal advice

JA Group Services Ltd does not provide immigration advice, visa advice, legal advice, nationality advice, residency advice, appeal support, refusal advice or representation.

Selected Partner Hotels

There are currently no confirmed selected partner hotels. Hotel-room-only bookings cannot be sold unless written commercial rights, supplier authority or partner permission is in place.

Changing information

Destination, entry, safety, health, local and provider information can change. Customers should check official sources and provider terms before making decisions.

Accessibility and personal needs

Accessibility, disability, medical, family or carer information is voluntarily provided by the customer and handled carefully. Provider accessibility information should be checked directly with the relevant venue, provider or hotel before relying on it.
