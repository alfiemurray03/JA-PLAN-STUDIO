# Administrator Guide

## CRM

- Use CRM to search customers, open full profiles, review flags, and inspect internal notes.
- Customer records should be treated as confidential and auditable.

## Support

- Use Support to manage case queues, assign work, update status, and track resolution.
- Internal notes are for staff only.

## Membership

- Use Membership to review lifetime access, complimentary access, upgrades, downgrades, suspensions, and cancellations.
- Review billing and audit history before changing entitlements.

## Notifications

- Use Notifications to draft, schedule, send, archive, duplicate, and delete communications.
- Use targeted delivery carefully and verify the audience before sending.

## GDPR

- Use GDPR to track subject access requests, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and consent withdrawal requests.
- Record deadlines, notes, attachments, and completion status.

## CMS

- Use CMS to manage public content such as homepage content, destinations, featured tours, affiliate pages, footer, navigation, contact, status, social tariff, plans, and legal pages.
- Publish only after review.

## Customer Management

- Use the customer profile workspace as the canonical internal record.
- Microsoft Entra identity data is read-only.
- Update only local operational fields and notes when required.

## Audit

- Review audit history regularly for support, membership, notification, CMS, and security actions.
- Treat audit records as the source of truth for operational traceability.
