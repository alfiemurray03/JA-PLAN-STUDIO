FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

Find clear answers about our guidance service, pricing, partner signposting and customer responsibilities.

Do you sell holidays or transport?

No. JA Group Services Ltd does not sell, arrange or package holidays, flights, visas, transfers, trains, taxis, car hire or other transport bookings.

Who books activities?

Activity bookings are completed directly with GetYourGuide and/or the relevant third-party provider. We may receive affiliate commission.

Are hotels bookable on this site?

Selected hotel-room-only information is provided for inspiration and planning review only. We do not activate live hotel payments.

Can I pay online?

Live payments are not activated on this website. Any future payment route will be clearly described in the relevant terms and pricing pages.
