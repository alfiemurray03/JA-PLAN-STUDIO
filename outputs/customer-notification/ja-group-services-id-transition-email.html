Dear User,

We are writing to explain an important change to the identity service used to access JA Group Services websites, portals and online services, and what this means for your account and personal data.

No action is required yet
The transition is still in progress. We currently plan to complete the final phase by 31 August 2026. We will contact you again when registration is available.

What is changing?

JA Group Services Ltd is replacing JA Group Services Secure Access, our former identity service, with JA Group Services ID.

JA Group Services ID uses Microsoft Entra External ID to provide a new, consistent and secure registration and sign-in service for participating JA Group Services websites, portals and online services. Until the work is complete, some services may display a coming soon, pre-launch or restricted-access page.

What will happen to your account?

  • Your former Secure Access identity will not be automatically migrated to JA Group Services ID.
  • When the new service is ready, you will need to register for a new JA Group Services ID account.
  • Your former password, authentication credentials, security tokens and sign-in secrets will not be transferred.
  • We will explain any secure process available for reconnecting access to applicable information held by individual JA Group Services services.
  • We will not automatically link records unless we can verify that they belong to the same person.

What will happen to your personal data?

We are reviewing personal data held in the former JA Group Services Secure Access tenant. This may include your name, email address or sign-in identifier, account or directory identifier, account type, identity-provider information and limited audit or sign-in metadata where required.

We will use only the information needed to notify affected users, administer the transition, protect our systems, respond to data protection requests and securely retire the former identity tenant.

Personal data will only be retained where there is a lawful and necessary reason. Information that is no longer required will be securely deleted or otherwise handled in accordance with our data protection obligations. Access during the transition is restricted to authorised personnel.

Why have you received this email?
An identity record associated with your email address was present in the former JA Group Services Secure Access tenant. This is an administrative service and data protection notification. It is not a marketing email, and it does not indicate that a personal data breach has occurred.
